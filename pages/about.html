About Me

I am a Master's student in Management Science and Engineering at Stanford. My official track is Technology and Engineering Management. Through this track, I have been able to take really cool classes on how stock markets work, how technology companies have set their strategies, hands on entrepreneurship class that led to Verix and a deep learning class by Andrew Ng which has by far been my favorite class at Stanford.

Apart from a lot of in school learning, I did an internship in Product Management last summer at ServiceNow. It was an incredible experience in terms of all the different things that I got to do ranging from analytics to UX to strategy. During this internship, I grew a lot not only professionally but also personally and this internship reinforced my interest in product management.

Before I came to Stanford, I worked as a Software Engineer at Visa in Bangalore, India with some of the smartest people I know building really useful products. I joined Visa after graduating form Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati with a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering. In my junior year at IIT Guwahati, I learnt the beauty of applied math in machine learning and thought I had found my passion in life. The rest of this website is dedicated to a lot of the things I like doing and that make me happy
